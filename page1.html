Our Company

Scarf Squad is renowned for its flawlessly designed and meticulously crafted bawal and shawls. Our mission is to stock your closet with hundreds of modest scarves you can wear every day. Our selection is versatile enough to be worn everywhere from a laid-back get-together to a formal workplace party.

As we say we desire to concentrate on the demands of all individuals searching for comfort but inexpensive scarves, we don't only mean ladies looking for modest wear.

We acknowledge that women want to seem stylish without sacrificing their modesty or their sense of style. We are pleased to be a part of the worldwide phenomenon that is modest dress.
We use only the finest voile fabric for our scarves, and each one is carefully silk-screened by hand.
All of you #scarflovers, be assured that our design staff appreciates what looks and feels outstanding on you.
